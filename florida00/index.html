Florida, 2000


This year's vacation travels took us to Florida, where we met with some family members and enjoyed the sun and warm weather.   The first few days were spent in Cocoa Beach, giving us the chance to relax while sitting on the beach or by the pool.  While there, we were also able to pay a visit to Kennedy Space Center.  The second half of the trip was spent at Disney World, endlessly jumping from park to park trying to pack every ounce of the Disney universe into the few short days we had there.
