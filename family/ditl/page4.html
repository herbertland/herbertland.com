A Day in the Life of Amelia!

And after lunch we went for a walk because it was such a nice day.  Daddy came home for lunch right as we were leaving, so he took our picture-- we were both really surprised to see his car pull up in the driveway & couldn't take our eyes off it!

 

The walk made me sleepy, so I took a nap.  Andy stayed up a bit longer than me, but he fell asleep too.

 

And when I got back home, I decided to make some music, one of my favorite things to do!

 

And after a while, mommy took us to play at the gym.  We play with other kids there while mommy works out.
