New Years Eve, 1999


New Years Eve 1999 was a busy one for us-- we arrived back from our trip in the mid-afternoon on New Years Eve.  After leaving the airport, arriving home, and feeding the cats, it was off to celebrate the coming of 2000.  We spent this New Years Eve with our friends the Fishels, joining them for dinner at Benihana and then to their house to ring in the new year.  You can click on any of the pictures above to see a larger image.
